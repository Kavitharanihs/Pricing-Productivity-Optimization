# Pricing-Productivity-Optimization
Pricing &amp; Profitability analysis using Python and Power BI
Problem Statement:
The business experiences strong sales but inconsistent profitability due to
discounting and pricing inefficiencies. The objective is to identify how pricing
strategies affect profit and recommend optimization actions.

Objectives:
- Analyze discount impact on profitability
- Identify loss-making products, categories, and regions
- Improve pricing and discount strategies

Dataset:
Sample Superstore Dataset (simulated retail business data)

Tools:
Python, Google Colab, Power BI, Excel

Analysis Summary:
- Cleaned and prepared retail sales data
- Performed exploratory data analysis on pricing and profit
- Built an executive-level Power BI dashboard

Key Insights:
- High discounts significantly reduce profit margins
- Certain categories and regions contribute to losses
- Profitability varies widely across products

Recommendations:
- Limit excessive discounting
- Focus on high-margin products
- Use region-based pricing strategies
